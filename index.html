About Me

I was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio and moved to St. Paul, Minnesota when I was 16. I would still consider Cleveland my home but I have grown to really like the Twin Cities and decided to stay for College. I'm not sure where life will take me once I graduate but I am currently enjoying my time in Minnesota.

Interests

I am a huge sports fan, especially baseball and football, but will watch pretty much anything that is on. Being from Cleveland, those are the teams I root for. I also enjoy playing video games and playing sports, like basketball or soccer, to stay in shape. I don't watch to many shows or movies since I often times lose interest mid way through and get bored, but I am a huge Seinfeld fan. I am very interested in art, especially modern art and have taken many art history classes. I also spend a lot of time reading and looking at architecture and fashion.
